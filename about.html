ABOUT ME

I am a Georgia native, living in Chicago for the last 4 years. After graduating with a business degree in Marketing and New Media Technology, I began my career in software sales at a high-growth startup, where I spent my days helping technical team members with complex search use cases across all verticals and industries.

I am passionate about equality in healthcare and wellness for women and working parents. I am the Chapter President for the Chicago PERIOD Movement, where we advocate for menstrual equity, ending stigma, and aid homeless populations in getting access to the products they need. I am also serve on the board at Between Friends where we assist victims of domestic violence in court processes.

I'm currently enrolled in the Flatiron Software Engineering Bootcamp, with an expected graduation of date January 2022. I am keen to marry my experience in the tech industry with my passion for women's healthcare, and hope to one day be able to help build technologies for the FemTech space.
